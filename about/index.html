About EoSApplications

A suite of tools for working with high-pressure equation of state (EoS) calibrations.

The Applications

EoSAlign

Convert and compare high-pressure experimental data across different equations of state calibrations.

EoSHolo

Explore the network of equation of state calibrations as an interactive graph.

Included Calibrations from the Literature

EoSApplications ships with over 400 calibrations spanning more than 30 compositions and three measurement methods (XRD, Raman, Luminescence). The superscript notation (e.g., 2) indicates the number of individual calibrations from that study.
